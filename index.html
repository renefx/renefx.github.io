Speaker - NSBRAZIL 2019

NSBRAZIL is the largest national iOS Development conference created by the Cocoaheads Brasil team. I had the opportunity to discourse about Core Data migrations - a topic not well explored in the community.

I based my presentation on a real case scenario in which the user-base fragmented in production. For the same app version, some users were in an older scheme of the local storage and others on the new.
